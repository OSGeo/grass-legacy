Image formats:

  • BIL: r.in.bin (.bil)
  • Binary files: r.in.bin
  • BSQ: i.tape.other (.bsq)
  • USGS DOQ: r.in.gdal
  • CEOS: r.in.gdal
  • GeoTIFF: r.in.tiff r.in.gdal (.tif/.tfw)
  • GIF: r.in.gdal (.gif)
  • JPEG JFIF: r.in.gdal (.jpg)
  • PPM/PNM: r.in.pbm, r.in.gdal (.ppm, .pbm)
  • PNG: r.in.png r.in.gdal (.png)
  • SAR/CEOS: r.in.gdal
  • SDTS DEM: r.in.gdal
  • SUNRASTER: r.in.sunrast
  • TIFF: r.in.tiff r.in.gdal (.tif/.tfw)
Raster GIS formats:
  • ERDAS Imagine: r.in.gdal (.img)
  • ERDAS/LAN: i.in.erdas (.lan)
  • ESRI/ARC-ASCII-GRID: r.in.arc (.asc)
  • ESRI/ARC-BINARY-GRID: r.in.gdal (.hfl)
  • ESRI/E00 (raster): m.in.e00 (.e00)
  • ETOPO-5: r.in.bin
  • GLOBE DEM: r.in.bin
  • GTOPO30: r.in.bin
  • GMT binary array: r.in.bin
  • NIMA/DTED: r.in.gdal
  • ESRI/SHAPE (raster): r.in.shape (.shp)
Vector formats:
  • AUTOCAD/DXF: v.in.dxf (.dxf)
  • AUTOCAD/DXF3D: v.in.dxf3d (.dxf)
  • DLG: v.in.dlg (.dlg)
  • ESRI/Ungenerate: v.in.arc (.lin, .pol, .txt)
  • ESRI/E00 (vector): m.in.e00 (.e00)
  • ESRI/SHAPE (vector): v.in.shape (.shp)
  • GSHSS: v.in.gshss
  • SDTS: v.in.sdts
  • MapInfo/MIF: v.in.mif (.mif)
Sites (point data) formats:
  • ASCII: s.in.ascii
  • ATKIS/DEM/KTB: s.in.atkisdgm, s.in.atkisktb
  • DBF: s.in.dbf
  • ESRI/SHAPE (points): s.in.shape (.shp)
  • MapInfo/MIF: s.in.mif (.mif)
